Simplify Your Digital Workflow

Most digital stress isn’t caused by technology itself — it’s caused by disorganization, overload, and too many disconnected systems.

Start with File Organization

A messy desktop creates mental clutter. Build a simple folder system you can trust.

  • Create clear folders by category
  • Name files consistently
  • Archive old files regularly
  • Use cloud storage wisely

Tame Your Email

Email chaos drains energy fast. Small systems make a huge difference.

  • Use folders and labels
  • Unsubscribe aggressively
  • Use templates for repeat replies
  • Schedule email time instead of reacting all day

Own Your Calendar

Your calendar should reduce stress, not create it.

  • Time-block important work
  • Add buffer time between meetings
  • Use reminders intentionally
  • Protect focus time

Use AI to Reduce Friction

AI can help simplify repetitive work.

  • Draft emails faster
  • Summarize meetings
  • Create task lists
  • Brainstorm ideas
  • Organize information quickly

Keep It Simple

Productivity isn’t about using more apps. It’s about building fewer, better systems that actually work for you.
