Why This Matters Now

Technology is moving fast — and that’s exciting. But for too many people, it’s moving faster than they feel ready for.

The gap isn’t just about having a device or internet. It’s about confidence and know-how. Many people — especially older adults, newcomers, and those on lower incomes — feel left behind by rapid changes and the rise of AI.

We built this program because everyone deserves to feel capable and in control.

Who This Program Is For

  • Older adults who want to stay connected with family and handle daily tasks online safely
  • Newcomers navigating Canadian services, jobs, and communication
  • Anyone who feels overwhelmed by technology and wants a clear, supportive path
  • Community organizations that want to bring digital confidence to the people they serve

No prior experience needed — just the willingness to start.

Your 8-Week Journey

Week 1: Digital Confidence Baseline & Tech Foundations
Week 2: Files, Cloud Storage & Digital Organization
Week 3: Online Safety, Scams & Password Security
Week 4: Everyday Productivity Tools & Digital Communication
Week 5: AI Foundations — What It Is, How It Works, and Where It Shows Up
Week 6: Practical AI for Everyday Life
Week 7: AI for Employment, Learning & Opportunity
Week 8: Building Your Personal Digital Toolkit & Next Steps

How It Works

You can move through the program at your own pace. Optional group sessions through local partners (libraries, community centres, etc.) are also available. Everything is plain-language, mobile-friendly, and supportive.
