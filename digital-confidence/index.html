Why This Matters Now

Technology is moving fast — and that’s exciting. But for too many people, it’s moving faster than they feel ready for.

The gap isn’t just about having a device or internet. It’s about confidence and know-how. We built this program because everyone deserves to feel capable and in control.

Who This Program Is For

  • Older adults who want to stay connected with family safely
  • Newcomers navigating services, jobs, and communication
  • Anyone who feels overwhelmed by rapid tech changes
  • Community organizations that want to support their members

No prior experience needed — just the willingness to start.

Your 8-Week Journey

Week 1: Digital Confidence Baseline & Tech Foundations
Week 2: Files, Cloud Storage & Digital Organization
Week 3: Online Safety, Scams & Password Security
Week 4: Everyday Productivity Tools & Digital Communication
Week 5: AI Foundations — What It Is, How It Works, and Where It Shows Up
Week 6: Practical AI for Everyday Life
Week 7: AI for Employment, Learning & Opportunity
Week 8: Building Your Personal Digital Toolkit & Next Steps

How It Works

Self-paced online + optional group sessions through local partners (libraries, community centres, etc.). Plain language, mobile-friendly, and supportive.
