Why This Matters Now

Technology is moving fast — and that’s exciting. But for too many people, it’s moving faster than they feel ready for.

The gap isn’t just about having a device or internet. It’s about confidence and know-how. Many people — especially older adults, newcomers, and those on lower incomes — feel left behind by rapid changes and the rise of AI.

We built this program because everyone deserves to feel capable and in control.

Who This Program Is For

This is for real people who want practical skills without the tech-speak:

  • Older adults who want to stay connected with family and handle daily tasks online safely
  • Newcomers navigating Canadian services, jobs, and communication
  • Anyone who feels overwhelmed by technology and wants a clear, supportive path
  • Community organizations that want to bring digital confidence to the people they serve

You don’t need any prior experience. You just need the willingness to start.

Your 8-Week Journey

A gentle, step-by-step path from foundational confidence to real-world AI use.

Week 1: Digital Confidence Baseline & Tech Foundations

Build your personal starting point. Understand the core ideas behind devices, apps, and the internet so nothing feels mysterious again.

Week 2: Files, Cloud Storage & Digital Organization

Learn simple, reliable ways to keep your photos, documents, and important information safe and easy to find — on any device.

Week 3: Online Safety, Scams & Password Security

Practical protection you can actually use. Spot common scams, create strong but memorable passwords, and browse with more peace of mind.

Week 4: Everyday Productivity Tools & Digital Communication

Make technology work for you — email, messaging, calendars, and simple tools that reduce daily stress.

Week 5: AI Foundations – What It Is, How It Works, and Where It Shows Up

Demystify AI. See where it already appears in your life and how it actually works, without the hype or fear.

Week 6: Practical AI for Everyday Life

Hands-on ways to use AI right now — for writing, learning, planning, creativity, and solving real problems you care about.

Week 7: AI for Employment, Learning & Opportunity

Use AI as a supportive tool for job searching, skill-building, resumes, interviews, and opening new doors.

Week 8: Building Your Personal Digital Toolkit & Next Steps

Pull everything together into a toolkit that fits your life. Leave with a clear plan for staying confident and curious long after the program ends.

How It Works

You can move through the program at your own pace, on your own schedule.

For extra support, many people prefer going through it together in small groups led by a trusted local organization (library, community centre, seniors group, or newcomer service).

Everything is built to be plain-language, mobile-friendly, and supportive rather than overwhelming.

What Makes This Different

This isn’t another generic tech course. It’s built with the same practical, human-first approach TechTidy is known for — starting with real people’s needs instead of shiny tools. We focus on confidence first, then skills.

The Impact We’re Building Toward

When people finish this program, they feel more capable in daily life. They spot scams more easily, connect with family without frustration, explore new opportunities, and approach AI with curiosity instead of fear.
